Common questions

Frequently asked questions.

How do payments work?

For most projects, I work with a 50% upfront deposit and 50% on completion. For larger projects, we can break it into milestone-based payments. All payments are handled through bank transfer or UPI for Indian clients, and PayPal/Wise for international clients.

What if the project takes longer than expected?

If delays are on my end, there's no extra charge. If the scope changes significantly during the project (new features, design changes), I'll provide a revised timeline and quote before proceeding. No surprises.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes! I work with clients worldwide. All communication is in English, and I'm flexible with time zones. USD pricing is listed alongside INR for international clients.

Can I see progress while you're building?

Absolutely. I share staging links and regular updates throughout the build. You're involved at every stage — nothing goes live without your approval.

What technologies do you use?

I use modern web technologies including React, Next.js, Node.js, and PostgreSQL/MongoDB. The specific stack depends on your project's needs — I always choose what's best for your goals, not just what's trendy.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes. Every project includes post-launch support (duration varies by plan). After that, you can opt for a monthly management plan for ongoing maintenance, or reach out anytime for one-off fixes.
